When Bangladesh Makes News in the West

For this research, I started by gathering relevant news articles from major Western outlets using a two-stage web scraping process. While I initially aimed to include top newspapers from ten Western countries, I decided to narrow the focus to the top three news websites from the US and the UK due to time constraints. I chose these sites based on SimilarWeb rankings to make sure they reflected mainstream media in these regions, and I concentrated on articles published from 2000 onwards to keep the data consistent and manageable.
